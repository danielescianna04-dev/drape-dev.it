Frequently Asked Questions

How can I cancel my subscription? ▼

You can manage or cancel your subscription directly from your iPhone Settings: Settings → [Your name] → Subscriptions → Drape. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.

Can I get a refund? ▼

Refunds for in-app purchases are handled by Apple. You can request a refund at reportaproblem.apple.com. For special cases, contact us directly via email.

My project won't start, what can I do? ▼

Try closing and reopening the workstation from the projects screen. If the problem persists, check your internet connection and try again. You can also contact us with the project details for quick assistance.

Which AI models are available? ▼

Drape supports 5 AI models: Claude 4.5 Sonnet, Claude 4.6 Opus, GPT 5.2, GPT 5.3, and Gemini 3.0 Flash. Available models depend on your plan (Starter, Go, or Pro).

How does the terminal work? ▼

Each project has a full Linux terminal with support for npm, pip, git, and other tools. You can run commands, install dependencies, and manage your project like on a computer.

Is my data safe? ▼

Each project runs in an isolated Docker container. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. We don't sell or share your data with third parties. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I delete my account? ▼

Yes, you can delete your account from the app settings (Settings → Delete Account). All your data will be permanently removed. You can also request deletion via email.
