FAQ

Pricing Questions

Can I try Drape for free? +

Yes! The Starter plan is completely free and includes Claude 4.5 Sonnet, Gemini 3.0 Flash, 3 projects, 2 cloned repositories, full terminal, and GitHub integration. You can use it forever without entering a credit card.

How does billing work? +

All payments are handled through Apple In-App Purchase. The subscription renews automatically and you can cancel it anytime from your Apple ID settings. There are no hidden costs.

Can I change plans anytime? +

Absolutely. You can upgrade from Starter to Go or Pro anytime, or downgrade at the end of the billing period. Every change is managed directly through the App Store.

Are there discounts for annual plans? +

Yes! With the annual plan you save 20% compared to monthly billing. Go annual costs €19.17/month (instead of €22.99), and Pro annual costs €33.33/month (instead of €39.99).

What happens if I exceed my plan's limits? +

If you reach the limit of projects, repositories, or previews, you'll receive a notification in the app. You can upgrade anytime to unlock more resources. You won't lose any existing projects or data.

Is there a plan for teams or businesses? +

We're currently working on a dedicated Business plan with advanced features for teams. Contact us at info@drape-dev.it if you're interested and you'll receive updates.
