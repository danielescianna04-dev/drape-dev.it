How to build an app from your phone in 2026

February 20, 2026
3 min read

Yes, in 2026 you can build an app from your phone. Not a toy app, but a real project: websites, e-commerce stores, dashboards, APIs, bots and much more. The secret? A mobile IDE with AI that does the heavy lifting for you.

Until recently, building an app required a computer, specific software and months of study. Today with cloud computing and artificial intelligence, your iPhone becomes a complete development studio.

What you can build from your phone

With Drape you can create virtually any digital project:

  • Websites and landing pages — Portfolios, business sites, one-pagers
  • E-commerce — Online shops with cart and checkout
  • Dashboards — Control panels with charts and data
  • APIs and backends — Servers, endpoints, databases
  • Bots and automations — Telegram bots, automated scripts
  • Blogs and CMS — Content-managed websites

How it works: 3 steps

1. Download Drape from the App Store and create a free account. In 30 seconds you have a complete development environment in the cloud.

2. Describe your idea to the AI in English or any language: "Create a website for my restaurant with menu, reservations and photo gallery". The AI generates the structure, writes the code and sets everything up.

3. Customize and publish. Watch the live preview, modify anything you want with AI assistance, and when you're satisfied publish your project online.

Do you need to know how to code?

No, it's not required. Drape's AI handles the technical side. You focus on the idea and the result. Of course, if you can code you can intervene directly with the built-in editor and terminal — you have full control.

Start building from your phone. Drape is free.
